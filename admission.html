Program Overview: Explore the dynamic field of computer science with our Bachelor's program. Gain a solid foundation in programming, algorithms, and software development.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Program Overview

Embark on a dynamic journey in our Bachelor of Science in Information Technology(BscIT)program.This comprehensive degree blends theory with hands-on experience, offering a diverse curriculum covering programming, cybersecurity, network administration, and more.

Admission Requirements: High school diploma (A minimum of aggregate 10) Minimum GPA of 3.0 A minimum of B+ in all DCIT courses
