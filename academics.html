ACADEMICS

1.Undergraduate Programs:

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science: Duration: 4 years

Overview: A comprehensive program covering core computer science concepts, programming languages, and practical applications. Specialization options include Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology: Duration: 4 years

Overview: Explore the intersection of technology and business with a focus on information systems, network administration, and cybersecurity.

2.Postgraduate Programs:

Master of Science in Computer Science:

Duration: 2 years Overview: A research-intensive program with specializations in Data Science, Human-Computer Interaction, and Distributed Systems.

Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Computer Science:

Duration: 3-5 years Overview: Pursue advanced research in areas such as Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Cybersecurity under the guidance of experienced faculty.

3.Research Centers and Labs:

Center for Advanced Computer Systems Research (CACSR) Data Science Lab Cybersecurity Research Group

4.Capstone Projects:

In the final year, students undertake capstone projects, applying their knowledge to real-world challenges.
